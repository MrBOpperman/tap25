Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ'S

WHO SHOULD APPLY?

- Individuals who have. just finish matric looking to start a career in Information and Communication Technology (ICT).

- Individuals who have just completed a Further Education and Training (FET) or University qualification in Information Technology (IT) and want to refine their skills, obtain internationally recognised certification and gain work experience.

- Individuals working in a junior ICT position and want to grow and advance their career in ICT.

- Anyone with a keen interest to accelerate their career ICT

WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS OF BEING PART OF THIS PROGRAM?

- Well firstly, the classes are online, this means you can attend your classes from wherever you want and since the classes are recorded, it also means you can catch up on work done/topics covered if you miss class for whatever reason.

- You will have a full opportunity to gain international IT certification from multiple leading technology companies such as Cisco, Fortinet and Huawei.

- You will receive Individual couching and mentorship to help you achieve your unique career goals.

- Once certified, your professional CV will be kept on our database and shared with a network of potential employers looking for individuals with your set of ICT skills and certification.

- Top performers will also receive Tech Conference vouchers and opportunities to represent Think Tank in global ICT competitions.

WHAT ARE THE PREREQUISITES TO QUALIFY?

- There are no prerequisites however some exposure Information and Communication Technology (IT), passion for technology and keen interest to pursue a career in IT would be advantageous.

- Training will be delivered online though our Live Virtual Instructor Led Classrooms. Learners can attend the virtual classroom from an location of their choice.

- The Live Virtual Classrooms are led by our internationally certified Virtual Instructors with years of industry experience in Information and Communication Technology (IT).

- You will also have access to the self-paced online learning content where you will have access to network emulator Software, engaging practical LABS and technical assessments.

- You will be able to update your online profile and connect with potential employers once internationally certified.

- You will also learn, network and work with other people through missions, assignment, competitions and conferences.

- Top performing learners will receive first priority for placement opportunities in our network of employers.

WHAT WILL I NEED TO ATTEND VIRTUAL CLASSES?

- You will need a reliable Laptop/Desktop computer with a reliable internet connection.

- Once successfully registered for the program, you will receive internet data from Think Tank for the duration of the program.

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST?

- The total cost for the programme is R82 500 (Including VAT) or R65 000 (Including VAT) depending on payment options selected. You may choose from the list of payment options presented in this brochure.

- You have the option to pay the full programme fee in cash or in instalments.

- If you pay through installments you will be required to pay a registration deposit to enrol in the program and later pay monthly debit order instalments (as per your selected payment option) for a period of 12 months.

- You are required to pay the registration deposit in full to be successfully registered.

- Please note the registration deposit is non-refundable.

THE PROGRAMME FEES COVERS THE FOLLOWING:

- Amazon Web Services (AWS) Cloud Foundation.

- Huawei Security Training + International Certification Exam Voucher.

- Fortinet Network Security Expert (NSE) 1, 2 and 4 + International Certification Exam Voucher.

- Cisco CCNA Routing and Switching + 50% Exam Fee Covered by Think Tank On Condition That You Pass Your CCNA R&S Final Exam On First Attempt On the Cisco Netacad Learning Platform.

- Access to Virtual Instructor Classes.

- Access to Recorded Virtual Classes.

- International exam readiness platform.

- Work Readiness Training (CV Writing, Interview Mastery and other soft-skills).

- The overall training runs for a period of Twelve (12) months.
